# Jocelyn Huang

Data Scientist | MS in Applied Data Science @ USC Viterbi

I am a data science enthusiast with a product mindset and a passion for real-world impact. My experience spans data labeling, SQL pipelines, LLM evaluation, and machine learning projects, where I enjoy building AI-driven tools that are both intelligent and user-friendly.

With a background blending business analytics and data science, I move seamlessly between technical depth and user-centered thinking. I've contributed to projects ranging from evaluating LLM responses in AI voice systems, building recommendation models with XGBoost and Spark, to training vision transformers for plant disease detection, and automating data pipelines with Python, SQL, and Django.

If you're building something in the AI space—especially with data, models, or users in mind—I'd love to connect!
